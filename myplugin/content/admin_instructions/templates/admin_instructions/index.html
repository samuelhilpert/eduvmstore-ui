Admin Instructions

Here you find numerous instructions for the admin.

Approve/Reject AppTemplates

Approve/Reject AppTemplates

  • Step 1: Navigate to the EduVMStore Admin panel.
  • Step 2: Expand the AppTemplate Management section.
  • Step 3: Find the Confirm new AppTemplates section.
  • Step 4: Search for the AppTemplate you want to look add, approve or reject.
  • Step 5: Optionally check out its details by clicking on the AppTemplate name.
  • Step 6: Click on the "Approve" or "Reject" button next to the AppTemplate.

Delete AppTemplates

Delete AppTemplates

  • Step 1: Navigate to the EduVMStore Admin panel.
  • Step 2: Expand the AppTemplate Management section.
  • Step 3: Find the Delete AppTemplates section.
  • Step 4: Search for the AppTemplate you want to delete.
  • Step 5: Optionally check out its details by clicking on the AppTemplate name.
  • Step 6: Click on the "Delete" button next to the AppTemplate and confirm the deletion.

Manage User Roles

Manage User Roles

  • Step 1: Navigate to the EduVMStore Admin panel.
  • Step 2: Expand the User Management section.
  • Step 3: Find the Edit User Roles section.
  • Step 4: Search for the User whose role you want to edit.
  • Step 5: Select the new role from the dropdown menu.
  • Step 6: Click on the "Confirm" button to save changes.

Delete Users

Delete Users

  • Step 1: Navigate to the EduVMStore Admin panel.
  • Step 2: Expand the User Management section.
  • Step 3: Find the Delete User section.
  • Step 4: Search for the User you want to delete.
  • Step 5: Click on the "Delete" button next to the User and confirm the deletion.
