Instructions

Here you find numerous instructions.

Create AppTemplate

Before proceeding to create an AppTemplate, you can search for existing AppTemplates to use or clone.

Create AppTemplate

  • Step 1: Open the EduVMStore dashboard.
  • Step 2: Click ‘Create AppTemplate’.
  • Step 3: Select an image from the dropdown.
  • Step 4: Enter a name for the template.
  • Step 5: Provide a short and detailed description.
  • Step 6: Add an instantiation notice (optional).
  • Step 7: Set available for All Lecturers or Creator only.
  • Step 8: Define minimal system requirements for the template.
  • Step 9: Select one or more fitting security groups.
  • Step 10: Set instantiation and account attributes.
  • Step 11: Write or upload a cloud-init script.
  • Step 12: Click ‘Create’ to finalize.

Launch Instance

Launch Instance

  • Step 1: Open the EduVMStore dashboard.
  • Step 2: Navigate to the AppTemplate Overview page.
  • Step 3: Click on the 'Launch' button.
  • Step 4: Enter a custom name for the instance if needed.
  • Step 5: Select the number of instances.
  • Step 6: Choose one or separate keys for the templates.
  • Step 7: Modify Flavor, Network, Volume and instantiation attributes for all or individual instances.
  • Step 8: Add users via attribute fields or CSV upload.
  • Step 9: Click on the 'Launch Instance' button.

Publish a private AppTemplate

Publish a private AppTemplate

  • Step 1: Open the EduVMStore dashboard.
  • Step 2: Search for your private AppTemplate you want to publish.
  • Step 3: Click on the dropdown menu next to the launch button and click on "Edit AppTemplate".
  • Step 4: Change "Available in EduVMStore for" to "All Lecturers".
  • Step 5: Click on the "Update" button.
  • Step 6: Your AppTemplate is now in the "Waiting for approval" state and will be published after the approval of the admin.

Clone AppTemplate

Clone AppTemplate

  • Step 1: Open the EduVMStore dashboard.
  • Step 2: Search for the AppTemplate you want to clone.
  • Step 3: Click on the dropdown menu next to the launch button and click on "Clone AppTemplate".
  • Step 4: Enter a new name for the cloned AppTemplate.
  • Step 5: Modify any settings or configurations as needed.
  • Step 6: Create the cloned template by clicking "Create".
