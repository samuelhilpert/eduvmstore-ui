EduVMStore is a platform designed for managing and provisioning virtual machines in an educational context. It integrates with OpenStack to simplify the creation, configuration, and management of VMs, enabling users, such as students and educators, to deploy customized learning environments quickly. The platform supports various VM configurations and resource allocations, making it suitable for classroom and research purposes.

The EduVMStore was developed by students of the DHBW Mannheim, Germany. The project was part of the Bachelor's degree program in Business Informatics. The names of the students are Valentin Czekalla, Jared Heinrich, Marian Rickert, Emese Racz, Monika Bircic and Samuel Hilpert.

EduVMStore is designed for students, educators, lecturers, and IT administrators in the DHBW that require virtualized environments for teaching, learning, and research.

An AppTemplate is like a recipe for creating virtual machines. Just as a recipe includes ingredients and steps to prepare a dish, an AppTemplate includes predefined settings such as resource requirements, network configurations, and optional scripts to create and deploy virtual machines.

It simplifies the process of provisioning virtual environments tailored for specific use cases, such as educational labs, research projects, or software development environments.
