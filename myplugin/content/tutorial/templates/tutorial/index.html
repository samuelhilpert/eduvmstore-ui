Create App Template

Follow these steps to create an app template:

  1. Go to the EduVMStore dashboard.
  2. Click on the ‘Create App Template’ button.
  3. Choose an image from the drop down menu.
  4. Upload or Write the required script for the image.
  5. Enter a name for the app template.
  6. Enter a description for the app template.
  7. Specify the version of the app template.
  8. Set the visibility to "Private" or "Public".
  9. Specify the required resources for the app template.
  10. Click on the ‘Create’ button to create the app template.

Start App

To start an app:

  1. Navigate to the app overview page.
  2. Click the "Start" button next to the desired app.
  3. Confirm the startup settings and click "Start".
×

Publish App

To publish an app:

  1. Ensure the app is complete and functional.
  2. Navigate to the publishing settings.
  3. Set visibility to "Public" and click "Publish".

EduVMStore is a platform designed for managing and provisioning virtual machines in an educational context. It integrates with OpenStack to simplify the creation, configuration, and management of VMs, enabling users, such as students and educators, to deploy customized learning environments quickly. The platform supports various VM configurations and resource allocations, making it suitable for classroom and research purposes..

The EduVMStore was developed by students of the DHBW Mannheim, Germany. The project was part of the Bachelor's degree program in Business Informatics. The name of the student are Valentin Czekalla, Jared Heinrich, Marian Rickert, Emese Racz, Monika Bircic und Samuel Hilpert.

EduVMStore is designed for students, educators, and IT administrators in educational institutions that require virtualized environments for teaching, learning, and research.
