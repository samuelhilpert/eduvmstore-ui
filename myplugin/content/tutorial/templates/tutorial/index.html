Create App Template

Create App Template

  • Step 1: Open the EduVMStore dashboard.
  • Step 2: Click ‘Create App Template’.
  • Step 3: Select an image from the dropdown.
  • Step 4: Upload or write the required script.
  • Step 5: Enter a name for the template.
  • Step 6: Provide a short description.
  • Step 7: Specify the app version.
  • Step 8: Set visibility to “Private” or “Public”.
  • Step 9: Define resource requirements.
  • Step 10: Click ‘Create’ to finalize.

Start App

Start App

  • Step 1: Navigate to the app overview page.
  • Step 2: Click the "Start" button next to the app.
  • Step 3: Confirm settings and click "Start".

Publish App

Publish App

  • Step 1: Ensure the app is complete.
  • Step 2: Go to the publishing settings.
  • Step 3: Set visibility to "Public" and click "Publish".

EduVMStore is a platform designed for managing and provisioning virtual machines in an educational context. It integrates with OpenStack to simplify the creation, configuration, and management of VMs, enabling users, such as students and educators, to deploy customized learning environments quickly. The platform supports various VM configurations and resource allocations, making it suitable for classroom and research purposes.

The EduVMStore was developed by students of the DHBW Mannheim, Germany. The project was part of the Bachelor's degree program in Business Informatics. The name of the students are Valentin Czekalla, Jared Heinrich, Marian Rickert, Emese Racz, Monika Bircic and Samuel Hilpert.

EduVMStore is designed for students, educators, and IT administrators in educational institutions that require virtualized environments for teaching, learning, and research.
